Fried Egg

How it's made

Make room for eggs in your diet. Here's how to fry the perfect egg with bacon fat, butter, or olive.

Ingredients

  1. 1 large egg
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil, vegetable oil, butter, or other fat
  3. Kosher salt, to taste
  4. Freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Steps

  • Gather the ingredients.

  • Heat about 2 to 3 tablespoons of fat in an 8-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat. Break an egg into a small bowl or ramekin.
    When the fat is hot but not smoking, slide the egg into it.

  • When the bottom of the egg looks opaque, tilt the pan slightly and use a spoon to scoop some of the pooling fat. Spoon the hot fat over the egg white.
    Repeat for about 2 minutes, or until the white is firm. Spoon some fat over the yolk until it is as runny or firm as you like it.

  • Use a spatula to move the egg to a plate. Season the eggs with some kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, and enjoy!
